Live Well

This page is designed to give you an insight into how the earth is being impacted by Global warming, pollution and food waste. You can also search for food recipes based on ingredients or dietary requirements, and visit the blog of the owner of The Little Green Larder, Jillian Elizabeth.

A healthy compromise for you and the planet is a diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables and plant-based protein sources including legumes, soybeans and nuts.
Global warming is projected to have a number of effects on the oceans. Ongoing effects include rising sea levels due to thermal expansion and melting of glaciers and ice sheets, and warming of the ocean surface, leading to increased temperature stratification. (Wikipidia)
Plastic takes a long time to decompose and can put toxic chemicals in our soil and water, which can have an impact on human health. Wildlife can also get entangled in the plastic or if they mistake it for food they can choke or starve to death.
